## Basic table

3 or more hyphens `---` for header

| Head 1 | Head 2 |
| --- | ----------- |
| Body 1 | Body 2 |

## Alignment

1 colon `:` for alignment

| Head left + placeholder | Head middle + placeholder | Head right + placeholder |
| :-------- | :---------: | ---------: |
| Body left | Body middle | Body right |
