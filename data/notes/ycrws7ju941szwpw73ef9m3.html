QR Decomposition

Reference: https://mml-book.github.io/book/mml-book.pdf

For any real matrix A∈Rn×n\bm{A} \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}A∈Rn×n can be decomposed into

A=QR\bm{A} = \bm{Q} \bm{R}A=QR

where Q\bm{Q}Q is orthogonal matrix, R\bm{R}R is upper triangle matrix

Linear system

Ax=bQRx=b\bm{A} \bm{x} = \bm{b} \\ \bm{Q} \bm{R} \bm{x} = \bm{b}Ax=bQRx=b

Such that

Rx=QTb\bm{R} \bm{x} = \bm{Q} ^ T \bm{b}Rx=QTb

which is easier to solve as R\bm{R}R is upper triangle matrix
