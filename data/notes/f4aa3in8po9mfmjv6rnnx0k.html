Surface SSS embedded in R3\mathbb{R}^3R3 can be locally approximated by tangent plane, orthogonal to normal vector nnn.

Normal curvature

For unit direction eθ\bm{e_{\theta}}eθ​ in the tangent plane, starting from the point where the tangent plane is evaluated, the normal curvature κN(θ)\kappa_N(\theta)κN​(θ) is defined as the curvature of curve that wraps eθ\bm{e_{\theta}}eθ​ onto SSS. (like how matrix exponetiation wraps vector on tangent plane to manifold)

Principle curvatures

Two extremum Normal curvature κ1\kappa_1κ1​ and κ2\kappa_2κ2​ (2 cause SSS is 2 dimensional manifold) with associated orthogonal direction e1\bm{e_1}e1​ and e2\bm{e_2}e2​.
