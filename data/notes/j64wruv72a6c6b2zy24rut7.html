Global fusion

Apply Incremental calculation (combine depth image one by one) pointwise for {p∣Fk(p)≠null}\{\bm{p}|F_k(\bm{p}) \neq \text{null}\}{p∣Fk​(p)=null} (unmeasurable region is not updated)

  • Unit weight is good enough
  • Truncate updated weight allow moving average reconstruction
  • Fuse raw depth map as smoothed one removes the high frequency structure
