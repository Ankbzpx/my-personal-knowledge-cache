> $\circ$ means composition of function

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\text{Product Rule:} \ \frac{\partial}{\partial } (fg)' = f'g + fg'
\\
&\text{Quotient Rule:} \ (\frac{f}{g})' = \frac{f'g - fg'}{g^2}
\\
&\text{Sum Rule:} \ (f + g)' = f' + g'
\\
&\text{Chain Rule:} \ (g(f))' = (g \circ f)' = g'(f)f'
\end{aligned}
$$
