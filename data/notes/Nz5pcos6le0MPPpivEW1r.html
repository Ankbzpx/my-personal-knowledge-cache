Adaptive Threshold

Similar idea: https://stackoverflow.com/questions/22583391/peak-signal-detection-in-realtime-timeseries-data

  • Apply AlphaFiltering to raw data
  • Apply Welford's online algorithm to compute online mean and variance from filtered data (ignore first few observations until statistics stabilized)
  • Detect using mean +/- 3 * std as threshold
